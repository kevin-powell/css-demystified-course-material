### Font sizes

#### Mobile

- all very small text: .75rem
- links and buttons: 1rem
- body: 1.25rem
- card titles: 1.375rem
- section titles and subtitles: 1.75rem
- primary title: 2.125rem

#### Large screens

- all very small text: .75rem
- links and buttons: 1rem
- body: 1.25rem
- card titles: 1.75rem
- section titles and subtitles: 2.125rem
- primary title: 4.25rem
